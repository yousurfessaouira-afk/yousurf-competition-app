Entry Allocation + Judge Mode

GKA-inspired entry allocation, waiting list, seeding, byes, bracket preparation, official role separation, and penalty validation. All data stays in this browser with localStorage; no backend, build step, npm, or external libraries.

Competition ready

Main event limit defaults to 24 per gender for Kite-Surf, Freestyle, Big Air, and Hydrofoil Big Air. Custom local limits are allowed and flagged as non-sanctioned / Race Notice settings.

  • Race Director controls competition conditions and heat status.
  • Head Judge controls judging decisions, corrections, penalties, and final result validation.
  • Judges score only and cannot edit confirmed scores.
  • Interference requires majority judge call before Head Judge penalty decision.
GKA-inspired rule basis from GKA RULEBOOK 2025.pdf

Used in this module: maximum 24 competitors per gender for Kite-Surf, Freestyle, Big Air and Hydrofoil Big Air; when entries exceed the maximum, selection is based on previous participation/ranking and/or kite merits, with video footage possible; local organizer wildcards require approval and published allocation; waiting lists are formed from pre-registered riders unable to gain immediate entry and ordered by seeding list, video applications, or wildcard category; a pull-out from a seeding-list place is replaced by the next rider by seeding list, a video application place by the next ranked video, and a wildcard place by GKA/local organizer decision; seeding for the first World Cup uses previous-year annual ranking results. Unclear or event-specific items are marked “Head Judge / Race Director decision required”.
