    GKA-inspired rule basis from GKA RULEBOOK 2025.pdf

    Used in this module: maximum 24 competitors per gender for Kite-Surf, Freestyle, Big Air and Hydrofoil Big Air; when entries exceed the maximum, selection is based on previous participation/ranking and/or kite merits, with video footage possible; local organizer wildcards require approval and published allocation; waiting lists are formed from pre-registered riders unable to gain immediate entry and ordered by seeding list, video applications, or wildcard category; a pull-out from a seeding-list place is replaced by the next rider by seeding list, a video application place by the next ranked video, and a wildcard place by GKA/local organizer decision; seeding for the first World Cup uses previous-year annual ranking results. Unclear or event-specific items are marked “Head Judge / Race Director decision required”.

    GKA-style Tour Ranking + Seeding Engine

    Annual standings, discards, seeding and audit trail

    Engine implements the supplied 2025 GKA/KWT-style logic: completed-event discard bands, cancelled/invalid stage exclusion, head-to-head and event-result tie breaks, progressive seeding weights, transparent explanations, and mandatory audit records for every manual correction. If an imported format cannot be mapped safely, the engine flags “Rule confirmation required by Head Judge / Race Director.”

    Text ranking import · CSV / TSV / spreadsheet paste
    Excel files are not imported directly in this MVP. Please export your spreadsheet as CSV or copy/paste rows from Google Sheets.

    Upload a text CSV/TSV file or paste copied rows from Google Sheets / Excel. The parser accepts comma CSV, semicolon CSV, tab-separated paste, messy headers, multiple event columns, stage points, placements, totals, empty cells, “-”, DNS, DNF, cancelled event columns, duplicate rider names, and UTF-8 names. Spreadsheet totals are never trusted; final rankings are recalculated internally with GKA-style discard rules.
